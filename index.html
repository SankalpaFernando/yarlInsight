Where You Learn Everything Essential for a Successful Tech Career

We are excited to introduce YarlInsight, an innovative Two-day Summer School dedicated to advancing skills in Software Engineering and Cloud Computing. Hosted by the IEEE Student Branch at the University of Jaffna, in collaboration with IEEE Young Professionals, YarlInsight aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands by offering an immersive educational experience.

Expert-Led Sessions

Learn from industry experts in various fields of software engineering and cloud computing.

Hands-On Learning

Engage in practical workshops to apply what you learn in real-time projects.

Comprehensive Coverage

From Node.js to Azure, gain knowledge across a broad spectrum of software engineering topics.

Networking Opportunities

Connect with peers and professionals to expand your professional network.

Career Guidance

Receive expert advice and guidance for advancing your career in technology.

Inclusive Environment

YarlInsight welcomes students, undergraduates, and professionals from across Sri Lanka, creating a vibrant and inclusive learning environment.
